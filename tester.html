Photo & Film

Since the Summer of 2015, I have been the photographer for the Lexington Historical Society. LHS is an organization that works to preserve the history of Lexington, Massachusetts. As the photographer for the organization, I have photographed the various historical sites in Lexington which the society helps to preserve. I also have photographed many of the events LHS holds nearly every weekend.

Being the photographer for the Lexington Historical Society is a very interesting job that allows me to combine my love for photography with my love for history while helping my community. Below, you can see some of the photos I have taken for the Lexington Historical Society.

In March of 2017, I spent about two weeks in India working with the Kilash Satyarthi Children's Foundation and Bachpan Bachao Andolan. These are two connected organizations started by Mr. Kailash Satyarthi who won the Nobel Peace Prize in 2014. Both organizations work to free children from child labor. Since Mr. Satyarthi began his work in 1980 he has freed over 86,000 children from child labor.

I heard Mr. Satyarthi speak at MIT after he had won the Nobel Peace Prize and was inspired to join his cause. Today I have photographed both Ashrams where the freed children are kept, all employees of the two organization, and I have made an employee handbook in English and Hindi.
